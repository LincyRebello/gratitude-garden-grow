## 🌿 About

Gratitude Garden is a daily mindfulness app where every gratitude entry grows a new plant in your personal garden. The more you practice gratitude, the more your garden blooms.

No overwhelming journaling. No long prompts. Just one seed, one thought, one day at a time.

---

## ✨ Current Features

- 🌱 **Daily Seed Planting** — write one gratitude entry per day and watch a new plant grow in your garden
- 🔥 **Streak Tracker** — tracks your current streak and longest streak to keep you motivated
- 🌿 **Plant Variety** — each entry grows a different plant so your garden always feels unique
- 🌍 **Garden View** — plants are placed naturally across the garden and fill up over time
-  📤 **Share Your Garden** — capture and share a snapshot of your garden on social media
  - 🔔 **Daily Reminder** — optional browser notification to remind you to plant your seed
- 📊 **Gratitude Dashboard** — analytics view showing your streaks, trends, consistency calendar and more

---

## 🚧 In Progress

This project is actively growing — just like the garden! New features are being added regularly:

- [ ] 🏆 **Streak Milestones** — special rare plants + celebrations at 7, 30 & 100 day streaks
- [ ] 🍂 **Seasons** — garden background changes with real-world seasons (spring blossoms, summer greens, autumn leaves, winter snow)
- [ ] 🦋 **Butterflies, Bees & Birds** — gentle creatures that occasionally visit your garden
- [ ] ☁️ **Moving Clouds** — softly drifting clouds across the sky
- [ ] 🌈 **Rainbow** — surprise rainbows that appear after long streaks
- [ ] ⏳ **Smart Lockout Message** — if you've already planted today, the app tells you exactly how many hours until              midnight when you can plant again
- [ ] 🌤️ **Day/Night Sky** — the background dynamically changes based on your time of day (morning, afternoon, evening, night)
